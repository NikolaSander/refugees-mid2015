Refugees and asylum seekers around the world in mid-2015

Explore the UNHCR's mid-2015 statistics on refugees and asylum seekers around the world.

Out of the almost 60 million people mentioned in the UNHCR statistics, only about 15 million are refugees who were granted asylum by the host country under the Geneva Convention (1).
More than 34 million people are internally displaced within their own country. Out of the stock of 15 million refugees, less than one million were granted refugee status during the first six month of 2015 (2).
The most recent flows are asylum seekers who have lodged an application, but have not yet been granted refugee status (3). In the first half of 2015, about one million people applied for asylum worldwide but only 43 percent of those were granted refugee status.

Click on a region to discover flows between countries. Flows/stocks with less than 2,000 people are not shown.
